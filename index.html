How to use

  1. In your phone settings, set the language to English.
  2. Choose a difficulty level (Easy, Medium or Hard).
  3. Tap Learn to start the flashcards.
  4. A verb appears — try to recall its forms.
  5. You have 2 seconds before the answer is revealed.
  6. Press the speaker icon to hear it pronounced.
  7. Press the arrow to go to the next verb.
