How to use

  1. In your phone settings, set the language to English.
  2. A verb appears — try to recall its forms.
  3. You have 2 seconds before the answer is revealed.
  4. Press the speaker icon to hear it pronounced.
  5. Press the arrow to go to the next verb.
  6. To change difficulty, go back to the home page.
